About

My name is Neil Lancaster and I have been designing websites for over two years as well as acting as a website administrator since 2011. The vast majority of the sites I designed in my first two years were in WordPress using the Divi theme before I began learning how to build websites from scratch. Since 2011, I have been managing my local cricket club's website - also built in WordPress. This is where I developed a passion for website design and development. Following university, I began my career at a local web design company.

After I designed a few websites, I sought ways of making them look more professional and engaging. This led me to start learning more CSS outside of work through online courses and trying out snippets of code on test websites. This site was built from scratch utilising the HTML and CSS I have learned inside and outside of my job. I am almost entirely self-taught but I am a fast learner and picked up the basics of design quickly in order to meet and exceed client expectations. This portfolio site uses HTML5, CSS3 and the Bootstrap front-end responsive framework (version 3.3.7, version 4 used in alternate portfolio below).

In the past 6 months, I have taught myself the basics of JavaScript (vanilla and the jQuery library) and PHP as well as learning the CSS pre-processor Sass. I am now developing projects to add to my portfolio using my newly learnt skills.
