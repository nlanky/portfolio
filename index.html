About

My name is Neil Lancaster and I have been designing websites for nearly two years. The vast majority of the sites I have designed have been in WordPress using the Divi theme. Since around 2011, I have been managing my local cricket club's website - also built in WordPress. This is where I developed a passion for website design and development. Following university, I started a job at a local web design company.

After I designed a few websites, I sought ways of making them look more professional and engaging. This led me to start learning more CSS outside of work through online courses and trying out snippets of code on test websites. This site was built from scratch utilising the HTML and CSS I have learned inside and outside of my job. I am almost entirely self-taught but I am a fast learner and picked up the basics of design quickly in order to meet and exceed client expectations. This portfolio site uses HTML5, CSS3 and the Bootstrap front-end responsive framework.
