Kamileon Lodges - WordPress

Kamileon Lodges is a pair of holiday lets in Cornwall comprising of a lodge and a chalet situated in a holiday park. The owners had attempted to build a basic website in WordPress to complement their TripAdvisor listings. They came to us wanting to centralise the booking and information on one new website. This presented a unique challenge due to the booking availability and discounts for the two properties.

The site was rebuilt in WordPress and used a plugin to deal with the booking system. It features a modern design for the rest of the website incorporating lots of call to actions. These hooks encourage visitors to check availability and contact the owners if they had questions. I presented the information on the individual accommodation pages so that it was easy to read and browse through, using icons for the amenities/features of the property. The site features customisation of the booking plugin (adding T&C checkbox on the booking form) as well as many CSS changes for design purposes.
