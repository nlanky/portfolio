Kynance House - Website Design & SEO

Kynance House is a Plymouth guest house situtated near the Hoe offering bed & breakfast. Their old website was made by Yell Business and looked dated and unprofessional. I updated their site with a full width look, adding interactive rollover elements and textured backgrounds.

It was also important that the website was optimised for Google and was designed to convert leads. There are many call to actions situated throughout the site and the contact information is prominent encouraging visitors to book a room. The website features custom HTML and CSS for the rollover effects, backgrounds on the home page (and throughout the site) and the home page slider. Media queries were used on the slider to make it responsive.
